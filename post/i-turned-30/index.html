I turned 30

Age has been just a number for me since I known things. Because it doesn’t mean anything, it doesn’t bring or guarantee anything, I can’t say I’m better, smarter or wiser than you because I’m older, then why do we care? I treat it a general guidance, I know its existence, I respect it, but I don’t serious follow it. Until I passed the 30 years old threshold few months ago, I distinctly feel things became different. Someone constantly whisper around my ears something like “you’re not young any more”, “your youth has passed”. I started feeling pressure to do something serious for myself the whole lifetime, otherwise I gonna be nobody and invisible as my 20s. Tho I still haven’t found what cause I could devote myself into, but I do notice lots of my ides or way of thinking has significant different with when I was young.

Time is limited, I’m more calm at most of things

I’m more patient on things I think worth doing. My belief has been constantly enriched since I left college, like freedom of speech, free market, digital resolution, individualism etc, my knowledge about my belief has been consolidated. I know what I’m doing, I know how the society is doing (roughly) and how it suppose to operate, knowing this give me so much confidence and assurance to do things I think it’s right. I’m calm, determined and relaxed, just do the right thing.

I’m more impatient on things I don’t think worth my attention. I started saying No a lot and I’m not sorry or unsettle about it, because I know what’s right for myself or the society overall. I became bold as I expected and wished, I’m happy about it. I did ignore lots of things or hurt/ruffle someone which I don’t really care, time is limited, and I’m worth being friend, people can’t appreciate my boldness and sincerity just let them get out of my way.

leadership

I constantly feel I’d like to be leader, because as an individual contributor the things I can do is so limited, since my time is limited, I can’t way that long. I’d like to leverage my knowledge/influence, take more responsibility, make things happen in a larger scale.

Give back

I’ve been a sucker a really long time and I’m shame of it. I’ve always absorbing from the society, people around me, I think it’s high time to give back.

As for the society, I’d like to make something good/nice/cool. I strongly touched the phrase Steve Jobs demoed in the presentation

Some people show their appreciation to the society by producing some product which is beautiful.

I really hope I could find a hell to die on, I’d like to burn myself in it, embody myself into the product, my knowledge, my values, my appreciation to the world.

As for people around me, I’d like to be an influencer, I’d like to be a mentor if I could.

settle down

I’ve been moving around a handful of industries and cities since I left college which was full of random and lacked of direction, I’ve been constantly saying goodbye for a lot of things, I thought I was cool and strong (not tied by anything), now I feel a little bit sad about it. I’m starting become old, things overdue need to be addressed. There is an old saying Chinese which I started feeling so wise.

有恒产者有恒心 which translate to English is “Who has stabled investment would have constant determination” shall have his peace of mind when he possesses a piece of land.

Now I’m little bit tired of wandering life. I’d like to find a place I could settle down, build a solid friend circle which could last long.
