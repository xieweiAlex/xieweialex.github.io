Looking for a partner in crime

Alex Xie

December 29, 2022

  • relationship
  • Romance

How it started

I’ve been alone for a while, and I’m getting old(30+), I started feeling the urge to have a “partner in crime”, AKA, feeling lonely. So I asked around, and people always ask me back “what kind of girl are you looking for? " I hate not being able to articulate what’s in my mind, so I decided to write it down since there is nothing secretive about it.

Basically, I’m looking for a girl who is just like me, I know it sounds quite narcissistic, but that’s my genuine feeling. Maybe because I’m quite self-contained and happy overall, I don’t want to break my inner peace, and I’ve seen some couples holding quite different values/lifestyles, and staying together just to get by, and unhappy, I hope not to be like that.

Of course, people are different, something that is easy for me might be difficult for another person. I can take compromises, this post is just a brain dump, and it can be taken as a reference.

Values I look for

Positive/optimistic

I’m an optimistic and happy person in general, and I value it. I live by hope, as opposed to fear, stress, or other stuff. I’m happy to see progress or improvement, that’s the primary drive for my day-to-day life.

It’s hard for me to work/live with negative people, or people who are over-pessimistic, even though I know sometimes their points are valid, but if there is no hope or action taking place, I would not enjoy whining about them.

Receptive / Open-minded

I’m from China, and as much as I love American/Western culture, I can’t deny that deep down I’m still Chinese. I think ideally she is an Asian descendant or at least into Asian culture.

I’m quite open-minded, and I enjoy learning new things(tools/ideas/concepts), and anything related to productivity and self-improvement. Not necessarily my partner needs to like/agree with whatever I’m pursuing, but I hope to see she has a similar mindset, ideally we can learn and grow together.

For example, in this year 2022, I’ve embarked on quite some explorations. Fermentation (Sauerkraut, Kombucha, kamichi, yogurt, etc), MBTI, and stoicism, just to name a few I’ve probably invested 100+ hours in each of them, and I’d love to talk about them anytime.

Supportive, feminine

My ideal partner would be compassionate, loving, considerate, etc, just some typical feminine traits I guess.
I’m not a “male chauvinistic” person per se, on the contrary, I see myself as a mild feminist. I like independent souls, but strong-minded/feisty females are not my ideal choice.

Lifestyles I look for

Abstraction vs Sensing

In MBTI’s parlance, I’m a “Ni” (Introverted Intuitive) person, I’m drawn into abstraction, as opposed to tangible materials/experience.

For example, my typical table topics are related to economy, business, power, psychology, tools(software, ideas, gadgets), etc. And I often found myself despising those material, superficial, sensual stuff/experiences. For instance: fine dining, clothing, “excessive cleaning” etc.

Bringing this up not to put it as a requirement but to seek understanding, I know lots of people are not like me, and I can surely understand. I hope my partner understands it as well.

Emotion

I’m quite emotionally stable, and I value it. I’ve seen many people who are not, and not even working on it, nor see it as a problem, that’s a big no-no to me.

I believe in stoicism(an ancient philosophy), the overarching idea of stoicism is accepting what you can’t control and having the courage to change what you can and having the wisdom to tell the difference.

Maintaining a consistent light mood is essential for me, I don’t expect everyone, myself included, could achieve that easily, we could work on that at least.

Money

I live quite modestly overall, I often joke that my spending capability is like a new grad, and I’m proud of it. I came from a humble beginning, I have always lived below my means, I guess it is probably applicable to most immigrants.

So I probably won’t be happy with people who enjoy extravagant (money-wise) lifestyles. I don’t mean we can’t indulge ourselves with some worldly pressure, we certainly can, but just do it sparingly.

I’m a big believer in investing, currently, I’m mostly invested in risky assets (stock and crypto), and I’d gradually move some of them to Real Estate and other safer assets. I hope my partner would not be a risk-averse person.

Active

I like all kinds of sports and workouts, I value maintaining a fit body, that’s the foundation of health. I hope my partner would enjoy physical activity as well, and we can do something like hiking, jogging, swimming, or kayaking in our spare time.

Physical Appearance

I’m not a saint, and I do like hot ladies. As for physical appearance, I value overall body shape and energy way more than the face.

In the end

There you have it, just primarily “Values” and “Lifestyles”, of course, there are lots of other things I care about, we can go from here.
