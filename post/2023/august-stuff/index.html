New tools

NAS

I’ve always known the importance of backing up data, and I’ve lost some important data(mostly about photos), but the frictions of plugging in and off a hard disk just put me off, thanks for Shane’s encouragement, I started exploring NAS.

NAS stands for Network attachment System, basically it hard-wired with Ethernet and provide the accessibility for any device in the same local network fore reading & writing, it’s that simple, but it opens lots of possibilities.

I should write a blog post about it.
